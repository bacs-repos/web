About Us

Bihabar Area Concern Society (BACS) is a non-governmental and nonprofit making organization founded in August 27, 2012 (B.S.2069/05/11) by the group of motivated social activists with a vision of Prosperous Nepalese Society by inspiring breakthroughs in the current development practice and creating pro-active community for sustainable development, peace and prosperity.
